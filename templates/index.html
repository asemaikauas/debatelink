About Us

DebateLink organization strives to popularize debates and critical thinking among the youth of Central Asia. We aim to make debates accessible for students from remote regions and underprivileged areas, offering full funding to participate in our programs. Additionally, we raise funds for important social causes by organizing charity debate cups.
